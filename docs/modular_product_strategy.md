# Modular Product Strategy: "Glass2Grid Flex"

## Addressing the Retrofit Market Gap

### 1. The Critical Market Gap

Most smart glass technologies (electrochromic, BIPV) require **complete window replacement**.

- **Problem:** 98% of the building stock for the next 20 years already exists. Owners will not tear out perfectly good double-glazing to install smart glass.
- **Gap:** Lack of non-intrusive, retrofit solutions for existing skyscrapers and homes.

### 2. The Modular Solution: Glass2Grid Flex & Tile

We propose splitting the product line into two modular form factors to maximize addressable market.

#### A. Product A: "The Retrofit Film" (G2G-Flex)

**Description:** A flexible, adhesive polymer film embedded with the waste-derived carbon dots.

- **Installation:** Applied to the _inside_ of existing windows (similar to window tinting).
- **Edge Concentrators:** The film directs light to a slim, modular "frame clip" containing the PV strips and battery/sensor unit.
- **Modularity:**
  - Cut-to-size rolls.
  - Clip-on power modules (can be swapped/upgraded without removing the film).
  - Wireless connectivity (Mesh network).

#### B. Product B: "The Smart Tile" (G2G-Tile)

**Description:** Small, hexagonal or rectangular glass tiles (e.g., 20cm x 20cm) that interlock.

- **Use Case:** Facades, semi-transparent partition walls, or greenhouse roofing.
- **Modularity:**
  - If one tile breaks, replace only that tile.
  - Scalable voltage: Connect more tiles in series for higher power.

### 4. Implementation Roadmap

1. **Phase 1:** Lab synthesis of Carbon Dots from Rice Husk (Completed).
2. **Phase 2:** Dispersion into PMMA/PDMS flexible sheets (Prototype).
3. **Phase 3:** Development of the "Power Clip" - a universal edge connector for the film.
4. **Phase 4:** Pilot retrofit on a university campus building.

### 5. Intellectual Property Strategy (Defensibility)

- **Process Patent:** The specific chemical pathway converting _High-Silica Rice Husk_ -> _Hydrophobic Carbon Dots_ suitable for polymer dispersion.
- **Design Patent:** The "Clip-on" energy harvesting edge connector mechanism.

### 6. Social Impact Verification (The "Hult" Factor)

To ensure the social mission is not just a marketing claim, we integrate a **Traceability Layer**.

- **Farm-to-Factory Tracking:**
  - Farmers register waste deposited via SMS/App.
  - Each batch of husk is geo-tagged.
  - Payment is released instantly via UPI (India Stack).
- **Impact Ledger:**
  - Investors and Building Owners can scan a QR code on the glass.
  - See exactly _which village_ the waste came from and _how much income_ was generated for that community.
  - This "Story in the Glass" increases the value proposition for LEED/WELL certified buildings.
